Butternut Squash Soup

Description

This top-rated butternut squash soup is basically fall in a bowl â€” smooth and creamy,
it's sure to warm you up from the inside on a brisk autumn day. This recipe uses a
handful of simple, affordable ingredients to create a standout soup that's more than
the sum of its parts..

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 1 medium carrot, chopped
  • 2 medium potatoes, cubed
  • 1 medium butternut squash - peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 (32 fluid ounce) container chicken stock
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat, and cook onion, celery, carrot,
    potatoes, and squash until lightly browned, about 5 minutes. Pour in enough
    chicken stock to cover vegetables.
  3. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce heat to low, cover pot, and
    simmer until all vegetables are tender, about 40 minutes.
  4. Transfer soup to a blender and process until smooth. Return to the pot and
    mix in any remaining stock to reach desired consistency. Season with salt
    and pepper.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
